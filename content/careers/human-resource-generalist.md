**Do you thrive in a fast-paced, accuracy-driven work environment where each day brings new opportunities to learn and grow? At Milk Source, our top-notch Human Resources team is dedicated to supporting one another and working collaboratively to achieve both individual success and organizational growth. We believe in fostering a positive and forward-thinking environment where continuous learning, shared goals, and mutual respect drive everything we do.**

Milk Source is seeking a self-motivated and experienced **Human Resources Generalist** to join our dynamic team. This vital role is full time and works 100% in the Milk Source Office in Kaukauna, WI. They will work collaboratively within the Human Resources team as well as with managers, employees, and other departments to provide support in multiple functional areas of Human Resources.

**Overall Responsibilities & Skills include:**

· Manage life-cycle recruitment process as well as onboarding, offboarding, transfers, and promotions processes.

· Benefits Administration including the management of our open enrollment processes.

· Workers Compensation case management, monthly safety analysis and reporting, and OSHA Compliance and reporting.

· Assist with Payroll Administration of 600+ employees.

· Preparing a variety of detailed reports and analysis to support our management team.

· Assist with development and implementation of employee appreciation events and initiatives across the company.

· Other duties as assigned.

&nbsp;

This role is perfect for a highly motivated, organized professional with a passion for accuracy and efficiency. If you or someone you know is ready to take on exciting challenges and seize the endless opportunities our team has to offer, we’d love to hear from you!

This role is 100% in office in Kaukauna, WI and includes competitive compensation & full benefits!
