MilkSource Genetics kicked of the 2026 show season with an impressive performance at the Midwest National Spring Show in Madison.

The team had Grand Champions in two breeds as well as the Reserve Grand Champion in a third and Honorable Mention Grand Champ in a fourth.

&nbsp;

**<u>Red &amp; Whites:</u>**

Ms. Ransom-Rail **Beth**\-RED-ET won Grand Champ, Senior Champ and 1st Production Cow. (Co-owned w/ Laurie Fischer & Ransom-Rail Farm).

MS **Be Tempted**\-RED-ET was the 1st Place Fall Yearling and Reserve Junior Champion.

Thornside Rager **Reba**\-Red won 3rd Place 4 Yr. Old.

&nbsp;

**<u>Brown Swiss:</u>**

Harvst Brz Els **Windstorm** was the Intermediate Champion of the Junior and Open Midwest Spring Brown Swiss Show in Madison, Wis.

She also received Honorable Mention Grand Champion and 1st Place Senior 3 Yr. OId.

&nbsp;

Jenlar Diego **Wedding** was the Reserve Intermediate Champion of Junior and Open Shows.

&nbsp;

**<u>Jerseys:</u>**

HIghway Balin **Journey**\-ET won Grand Champion, Senior Champion and 1st Production Cow.

Milksource **Miss America** earned 2nd Place Spring Junior 2 Yr. Old.

Homeridge Malibu **Viv** Two captured 2nd Place 5 Yr. Old.

&nbsp;

**<u>Holsteins:</u>**

Eixdale **PWRUP** Alongside won Res. Grand Champion, Res. Senior Champion and 2nd 5 Yr. Old.

Jacobs Chief **Dina** was 1st 4 Yr. Old.

Milksource Avenger **Cazadores** was 2nd Fall Calf.
